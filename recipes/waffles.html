Waffles

This waffle recipe is the only one you'll ever need to know.

This simple recipe uses basic ingreidents to create great waffles quickly.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Non stick cooking spray
